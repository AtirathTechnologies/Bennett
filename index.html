Domestic Students

Applicants must have Passed Graduation in any discipline from a UGC-recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks and English as medium of instruction.

International Students

The eligibility criterion for all programmes for international applicants is minimum 50% in the qualifying examination and having studied the prerequisite subjects for admission into the desired programme.
